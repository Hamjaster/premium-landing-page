# Project Design Context

## Users
- **Audience**: Visitors evaluating a premium natural skincare brand.
- **Context**: They expect a clean, fresh, and trustworthy first impression that signals natural quality and scientific credibility.
- **Job to be done**: Understand the brand's natural philosophy and feel confident in the products.
- **Emotional goals**: Trust, calm, natural beauty, and wellness.

## Brand Personality
- **Voice**: Warm, knowledgeable, and approachable without feeling clinical.
- **Tone**: Natural, refined, modern, and nurturing.
- **3-word personality**: Natural, Refined, Trustworthy.
- **Emotional goals**: Connection to nature, self-care confidence, and brand loyalty.

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone**: Light, airy, natural, and premium. Soft organic shapes with clean typography.
- **Primary colors**: Warm cream (#FDF8F0), Sage green (#5C7A62), Cream accent (#E8D5B7).
- **Typography**: Satoshi family with strong hierarchy and restrained contrast.
- **Theme**: Light mode only.
- **References**: Aesop, Glossier, The Ordinary, Kiehl's.
- **Anti-references**: Dark themes, neon colors, cluttered interfaces, aggressive marketing styling.

## Design Principles
1. **Nature first**: Treat each section like a breath of fresh air — clean, open, and natural.
2. **Whitespace over decoration**: Use generous spacing and soft gradients to build calm, not visual noise.
3. **Typography leads**: Headlines carry the message and visual weight with natural elegance.
4. **Soft layers and contrast**: Use subtle glass layers and warm borders to separate surfaces gently.
5. **Motion with purpose**: Animate only when it clarifies or elevates the experience.
6. **Measured rhythm**: Keep spacing, alignment, and transitions disciplined and organic.

## Implementation Constraints
- **Framework**: React + TypeScript + Vite.
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS only for future UI work in this project.
- **Animations**: Framer Motion and GSAP are available if needed, but should stay restrained.
- **Font**: Satoshi via Fontshare CDN.
- **Theme**: Light mode only.

## Color Tokens
- **Backgrounds**: bg-primary (#FDF8F0), bg-secondary (#F5EDE0), bg-tertiary (#EDE3D5).
- **Sage accents**: sage-primary (#5C7A62), sage-light (#7A9E82), sage-dark (#3D5A44).
- **Cream accents**: cream-accent (#E8D5B7), cream-light (#F2E6D0), cream-dark (#D4B896).
- **Text**: text-primary (#2D2D2D), text-secondary (#6B6B6B), text-tertiary (#9A9A9A), text-sage (#5C7A62).
- **Borders**: border-subtle, border-medium, border-strong.
- **Glass**: glass-bg, glass-border.
